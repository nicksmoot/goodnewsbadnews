# Product Brief

## Brand line

**Good News Bad News — What residents are seeing.**

## Product mantra

**See it. Verify it. Solve it.**

## Purpose

Help residents share verified community signals without becoming a gossip site or outrage network.

## MVP capabilities

- Users can sign up through Supabase Auth.
- Users can submit signals into a safety-first workflow.
- Moderators can approve signals.
- Published signals appear in city and neighborhood feeds.
- Users can subscribe through Stripe.
- Subscribers receive a Saturday digest through Resend.

## Safety rules

Never allow doxxing, minor names, private medical information, unverified allegations, threats, or harassment.

## Civic Signal Score

Score range: 0–100. Factors include verified submissions, helpful votes, corrections, and resolved signals.
