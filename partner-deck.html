Why it compounds 08 / 09
The reason behind all of it

Pay the writer,
and the supply grows itself.

Legacy local news broke because the reporting cost was fixed and the revenue kept falling. We invert it. Every dollar a publisher spends licensing a story flows back to the resident who reported it, which pulls in the next reporter. The more you license, the deeper the coverage gets.

Community goodwill is the byproduct. A city that gets paid to tell its own story trusts the paper that carries it.
